Recommendations

Action Plan
🔌

Upgrade Grid Infrastructure

10 of 25 points face frequent outages with 5 more reporting unpredictable supply. Authorities should prioritise substation upgrades and line maintenance in affected clusters.

🔴 High Priority
🔋

Expand Backup Power Solutions

8 of 25 households already use generators. Community-level solar microgrids or battery systems should be encouraged for the remaining 17 to reduce vulnerability.

🟡 Medium Priority
💡

Fix Non-Functional Streetlights

4 of 25 locations report non-working streetlights. Targeted maintenance in these zones will improve safety and align with SDG 11 targets for sustainable cities.

🔴 High Priority
📊

Expand Survey Coverage

The 25-point survey provides a solid neighbourhood baseline. Expanding to 50+ points across multiple wards will yield statistically significant data for policy.

🔵 Future Action
🌱

Integrate Renewable Sources

Incentivising rooftop solar adoption will align the area with SDG 7 targets and reduce dependence on an already-strained government grid.

🟡 Medium Priority
📡

Deploy Smart Metering

Smart meters will enable real-time outage detection, faster utility response, and a longitudinal dataset for tracking electricity access over time.

🟢 Long-term
